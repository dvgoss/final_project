About Me

Hi! I'm Deja!

I'm a third-year student at the University of Michigan, pursuing a degree in UX within the School of Information. With a passion for creating intuitive and impactful user experiences, I'm dedicated to honing my skills in design, research, and problem-solving.
My journey into UX was inspired by my background in nonprofit work, where I witnessed firsthand the power of technology to drive positive change. Through my studies and experiences, I'm eager to leverage my unique blend of empathy, creativity, and analytical thinking to craft solutions that enhance the way people interact with technology and each other.
